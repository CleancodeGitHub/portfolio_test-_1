Humber College Virtual Tour

U/X DESIGN


As part of Humber College's Multimedia Design and Developement program final semester internship, myself and four other students developed a UX Design experience for a Humber College Virtual Tour in reaction to the COVID-19 pandemic.

We began by conducting interviews with various stakeholders such as prospective students, current students, parents and faculty. We then synthesized our research data to develop target personas and new concepts based on the needs identified.

From there, we created wireframes, low-fidelity landing page prototypes and email templates, crafting and testing user-friendly solutions across various digital platforms.

Throughout the process, we continuosly checked in with our internship advisor, collecting feedback and making adjustments along the way. Our final presentation was well received and we were invited to present our project to the Dean of Humber College.
